AI Interview Screener

Professional AI-powered interview evaluation system.

How to use:

Enter an interview question and a candidate's answer below. The AI will evaluate it and return a score (1-5), a brief summary, and a suggestion for improvement.

How to use:

Enter an interview question and multiple candidate answers, separating each candidate's answer with `---` (three hyphens) on a new line. The AI will evaluate all of them and return a ranked list.
